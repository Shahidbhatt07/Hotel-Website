Unique Dining

OUR THING IS FIGURING OUT HOW TO MAKE YOUR THING HAPPEN, NO MATTER WHAT’S HAPPENING. Making sure life’s most-important celebrations are as awesome as possible is our job at Unique Dining. And we REALLY love our job. We pride ourselves on making things work, and making them work well. Our goal is to be a problem-solving, magic-making partner to our clients as we serve meals to corporate offices, at weddings and events across Minnesota and at our HQ venue, Courtyards of Andover. We also craft a mean drink with our licensed bartending service. We care A LOT about our clients, our team, and our community. COVID-19 has been our biggest challenge as a company. It hasn’t been easy figuring it all out, but we think we’ve done a pretty good job, safely and smartly helping folks figure out what events look like right now. We celebrate EVERY DAY these days, grateful to be working together, with you, to celebrate the BIGGEST DAYS of your life.
